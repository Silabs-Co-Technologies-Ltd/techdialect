# Techdialect: Winning the ₦10 Million Challenge (Zero-Budget Strategy)

This strategy focuses on maximizing **Impact**, **Traction**, and **Social Proof** using only our current free infrastructure (SQLite, Flask, PythonAnywhere, HuggingFace Free Tier).

---

## 1. The "Lean & Mean" Technical Edge
We don't need a massive cloud architecture to win; we need a system that **works perfectly** and **solves a real problem**.

*   **Database Reliability**: We have implemented `PRAGMA journal_mode=WAL` and optimized indexes. This allows our SQLite database to handle hundreds of concurrent users without crashing—proving we can scale without high costs.
*   **Data Quality as a Feature**: Instead of paying for linguists, we will leverage the **Badge System** to identify "Top Contributors" and give them "Moderator" status to verify translations. This creates a self-cleaning dataset.
*   **Performance Optimization**: By pre-computing badges and using fuzzy matching, we keep the site fast even on the PythonAnywhere free tier.

---

## 2. High-Impact Growth (Zero Cost)
The judges care about **traction**. How many people are using it?

*   **University Outreach**: Partner with Language Departments at UNN (Igbo), UI (Yoruba), and BSU (Tiv). Students can use Techdialect for projects, generating thousands of translations for free.
*   **The "Badge" Social Flywheel**: Our shareable badge cards (`/badge/<username>`) are designed for WhatsApp and Twitter. We will encourage users to share their "Legend" status to drive viral growth.
*   **STEM Terminology Focus**: There is a massive gap in local language STEM (Science, Tech, Engineering, Math) content. By focusing our community on these 40+ STEM categories, we make Techdialect indispensable for education.
